## Privacy & Non-goals

This project is intentionally not designed for surveillance or enforcement. Specifically, it does not support:
- identifying or “warranting” people’s identities
- biometric identification or tracking
- GPS/location tracking or camera-grid aggregation
- covert capture, intimidation, or “making people nervous”
- claims about wrongdoing based on audio/video cues
